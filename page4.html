🌙 The Little Things

Sometimes it's the smallest things that stay with us the longest.

The way you laugh when something is genuinely funny.
The way you tell stories and somehow make every detail interesting.
The way a random message from you can completely change the mood of a day.
The way you don't even realize how much you mean to the people around you.
Funny how the smallest things often become the most memorable.
And somehow, all those little things add up to someone pretty special.
